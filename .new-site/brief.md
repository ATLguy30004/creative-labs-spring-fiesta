# Brand Brief — Creative Labs Spring Fiesta

## Positioning
- **Business:** Creative Labs Center — early childhood education center (daycare) in Alpharetta, GA
- **Ideal Customer:** Parents of currently enrolled Creative Labs students
- **Primary CTA:** Informational — no action required; just ensure parents know the event details
- **Differentiator:** Family-feel environment; this event celebrates their community with a collaboration partner (Elote King Atlanta)
- **Positioning Statement:** Creative Labs is hosting a Spring Fiesta on March 28 in collaboration with Elote King Atlanta. Enrolled students each get 2 free tickets redeemable for a Popsicle or Mechanical Bull ride at Elote King. Parents need all the details in one place — date, time, location, what to expect, and what's free.

## Brand Voice & Tone
- **Personality:** Warm, celebratory, community-focused
- **Tone:** Friendly and inviting — like a parent talking to another parent
- **We are:** Festive, inclusive, family-first, local
- **We are NOT:** Corporate, formal, generic event-promo

## Visual Identity

### Color Constraints
- **Anchor colors:** Creative Labs Green `#71AF47` · Creative Labs Yellow `#FFD024` — present in all directions
- **Avoid:** Cold blues, corporate grays, muted/desaturated palettes

### Font Constraints
- None — designer's choice per concept (Baloo Chettan is in Creative Labs brand stack; use where it fits)

> Final palette and fonts are selected per concept direction.

### Visual Style
- **Keywords:** Festive, springtime, community, vibrant, fun
- **Inspiration patterns:** Mexican-inspired fiesta energy + family-friendly spring event
- **Do:** Energetic use of color, celebration, warmth, clear hierarchy on event details
- **Don't:** Generic event flyer aesthetics, cold palettes, excessive formality
