# Your New Site Plan — Creative Labs Spring Fiesta

## Brand Brief
Creative Labs Center (Alpharetta, GA daycare) is hosting the **Spring Fiesta** on Saturday, March 28, 7–11 PM in collaboration with Elote King Atlanta. The landing page is purely informational — targeted at parents of enrolled students. Each enrolled student gets 2 free tickets redeemable for a Popsicle or Mechanical Bull ride at Elote King. Food is available from Elote King, and parking is on-site. Creative Labs' brand anchors: green `#71AF47` and yellow `#FFD024`.

## Visual Direction
- Color/font constraints: Anchor green `#71AF47` + yellow `#FFD024` in every direction. No required font — Baloo Chettan (brand font) used in Direction 1.
- Vibe: Festive · Springtime · Community · Vibrant · Warm

## Concept Directions

### Direction 1: Fiesta Viva
**Palette:** Fiesta Green `#71AF47` · Sunshine Yellow `#FFD024` · Coral `#FF5C35` on Cream `#FFF8F0`
**Fonts:** Baloo 2 + Nunito

Chunky, dense, and celebratory — a Mexican fiesta poster. Massive typographic hero, colorful event-detail tiles, confetti texture, rounded pill shapes throughout. Baloo 2 brings brand continuity while the coral adds heat.

### Direction 2: Spring Garden Party
**Palette:** Leaf Green `#71AF47` · Honey `#FFD024` · Mango `#FF9E4F` on Soft Mint `#F4FFF0`
**Fonts:** Fraunces + DM Sans

Airy, organic, and warm — a garden party invitation. Generous whitespace, wave section dividers, illustrated spring elements in the hero. Approachable and family-first.

### Direction 3: Noche de Fiesta
**Palette:** Neon Yellow `#FFD024` · Verde `#71AF47` · Hot Pink `#FF2D6B` on Near-Black `#0E0E0E`
**Fonts:** Raleway + Outfit

High-contrast, electric, night-event energy. Dark background makes the yellow and green explode. Festival-poster grid layout. Hot pink badge for the free tickets callout. Built for 7–11 PM.
